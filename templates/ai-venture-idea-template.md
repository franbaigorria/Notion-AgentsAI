# Idea Name

## One-Line Pitch

What is the simplest way to describe the product in one sentence?

## Problem

- What pain does it solve?
- For whom?
- Why is the current alternative weak?

## User And Buyer

- Primary user
- Economic buyer
- Initial market or niche

## MVP

- Core workflow
- What must work on day one
- What is explicitly out of scope

## AI Layer

- Model behavior required
- Whether this needs RAG, memory, tool use, or voice
- Latency expectations
- Reliability and hallucination risks

## Product Notes

- Tone, UX, or language constraints
- Why this would feel valuable to the user
- What makes the experience hard to copy

## Use Cases

- Immediate practical use cases
- Good early adopters
- Long-term expansion path

## Research

### Market Signals

- What evidence suggests the problem is real?
- Is this a growing category or a niche need?
- What adjacent behavior already exists?

### Alternatives And Competitors

- How is this solved today?
- Which tools, agencies, or internal workflows compete with this?
- What would make users switch?

### Technical Notes

- What technical constraints matter most?
- What is hard in practice: latency, memory, retrieval, evaluation, integrations, voice quality?
- What should be validated before overbuilding?

### GTM Notes

- Who is easiest to sell this to first?
- What channel would likely acquire the first users?
- Is the buyer the same as the daily user?

## Risks

### Technical

- Model quality
- Data availability
- Evaluation
- Infra cost

### Business

- Willingness to pay
- Replacement by incumbents
- Sales friction
- Compliance or trust issues

## Suggested Repo

- Repo name
- Main service boundaries
- Likely stack or constraints

## Open Questions

- What do we still need to validate?
- What assumption is most dangerous?

## Next Step

- Best next experiment this week
- What proof would make this worth building
